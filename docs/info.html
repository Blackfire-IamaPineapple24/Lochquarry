Information


Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in acres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll hills. On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery, but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities.


With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people, Lochquarry truly bring adventure to everyone.


Each activity is run under instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided.
